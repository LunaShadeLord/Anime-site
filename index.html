Anime


Anime—Japanese animation. Unlike cartoons from other countries, intended mainly for viewing by children,
Most of the anime produced is aimed at teenage and adult audiences, and largely due to this, it is very popular in the world.
Anime is distinguished by a characteristic manner of drawing characters and backgrounds.
It is published in the form of television series, as well as films distributed on video media or intended for film screening.
Plots can describe many characters, differ in a variety of places and eras, genres and styles.

Mangas


Manga—Japanese comic books,
sometimes called komikku. Manga in the form in which it currently exists began to develop after the end of World War II.
heavily influenced by Western traditions. However, manga has deep roots in early Japanese art. In Japan, manga is read by people of all ages. Manga is respected
as a form of fine art and as a literary phenomenon.
